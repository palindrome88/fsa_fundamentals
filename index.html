Dilled Chicken with Mushroom and Rice

Here's a comforting chicken and rice dish for your repertoire, and this one has only a single pan to wash.

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat a 12-inch nonstick skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Remove from heat and spray with vegetable oil spray. Add onions and cook for 5 minutes, or until translucent, stirring frequently.

  2. Meanwhile, rinse chicken and pat dry with paper towels. If using tenderloins, cut into strips about 1/2 inch wide.

  3. Add chicken, mushrooms, water, rice dillweed, and 1/2 teaspoon salt to onions, stirring well; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 20 minutes, or until water is absorbed and rice is tender. Remove from heat.

  4. Stir in lemon juice and 1/4 teaspoon salt.

  5. Drizzle with olive oil, then sprinkle lightly with paprika.
